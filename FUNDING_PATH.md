## Phase 1: Month 3-4 — Apply to Everything

### SC SCRA (South Carolina Research Authority)
- **SC Launch:** $50-200K investment for SC-based startups
- **Apply at:** sclaunch.com
- **Requirements:** SC-based (you qualify), technology company, early stage
- **Advantage:** Almost no competition in AI infrastructure from SC. You'd stand out immediately.
- **Timeline:** Rolling applications, 4-6 week review

### SC SBIR/STTR
- **Federal grants** administered through SC channels
- $50-250K Phase I grants for R&D
- Agent governance = cybersecurity = defense-relevant
- Apply through sbir.gov, mention SC nexus

### Innovate SC
- State-level startup support
- Connections to angel investors in Charleston, Greenville, Columbia

### YC (Y Combinator)
- **Apply:** ycombinator.com/apply
- **Batch:** S27 (Summer 2027) or W27 (Winter 2026-27)
- **Investment:** $500K for ~7% equity
- **What they want to see:** Working product + traction ($5K+ MRR ideal)
- **Your pitch:** "Built the governance layer for AI agents. 30+ API endpoints, proxy mode, 98/100 Smithery. $5K MRR growing 30%/mo. Solo founder, nights and weekends."
- **Timeline:** Apply month 3-4, interview month 4-5, batch starts month 5-6

### Other accelerators to apply to simultaneously:
- **Techstars** — various programs, some remote
- **Neo** — for technical solo founders
- **Pioneer** — fully remote, weekly tournaments
- **Antler** — global, some US cohorts
- **500 Global** — large network

**Apply to all of them.** Costs nothing. Increases odds.
